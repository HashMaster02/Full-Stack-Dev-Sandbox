Austin Kleon

Austin Kleon is the New York Times bestselling author of a trilogy of illustrated books about creativity in the digital age: Steal Like An Artist, Show Your Work!, and Keep Going.

  • In previous lives, he worked as a librarian, a web designer, and an advertising copywriter.
  • He's been featured on NPR's Morning Edition, PBS Newshour, and in The New York Times and The Wall Street Journal.
  • He speaks for organizations such as Pixar, Google, SXSW, TEDx, and The Economist.
